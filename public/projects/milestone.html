Overview

The Milestone app is designed for student drivers to help them track their driving hours and meet state requirements. It includes a tracking system that allows for real-time logging and reviewing of past driving hours, as well as statistics to monitor progress. Additionally, the app provides a comprehensive study section covering important concepts needed to pass a driver's license test.
