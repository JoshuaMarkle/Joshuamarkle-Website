    Milestone 🚗

    A mobile app designed to help student drivers earn their drivers license

    📖 Overview

    Milestone is the ultimate app for student drivers who want to take control of their driving journey. With its powerful tracking system, Milestone makes it easy to log your driving hours and ensure that you meet the minimum requirements set by your state✅. Plus, with the ability to review your past driving hours and get detailed statistics, you can easily understand your progress and set new goals🎯 for yourself.

    📑 Tracking Hours

    One of the key features of Milestone is its powerful tracking system, which allows you to log your driving hours in real-time. This makes it easy to keep track of your progress and ensure that you are meeting the minimum requirements for your state. You can also review your past driving hours and a simple collection of statistics to help you understand your progress.

    When it comes to tracking driving hours, there are three primary methods available to users: Live Sessions, Past Sessions, and Templates✨.

    Live Session

    During a Live Sessions, users will have access to a timer⌛ that can track their driving in realtime. And when they complete their drive, they can simply end the timer and all the information about the drive will be logged automatically.

    Past Session

    Users can input information about their past drives and log hours at any time. When doing so, they will be prompted to provide details📄 such as the date, weather conditions, start and end times, and any notes they want to add.

    Templates

    Milestone offers a unique feature that allows users to create Templates, which can save time and streamline🌶️ the logging process. For instance, if a user frequently drives from one location to another, they can set up a Template for that particular route and avoid the need to manually enter the same information repeatedly.

    🗝️ Key Information

    But Milestone is much more than just a tracking app. It also includes a comprehensive study section that covers all the key concepts you need to know in order to get your driver's license. From traffic laws to road signs, Milestone has everything you need to pass your test with flying colors.
